About Us

Purpul Studios was founded by Marcus Tinney, otherwise known as PurpulPancakes online. He grew up loving video games, starting with a Nintendo 64 and quickly loving every second of it. He was greatly inspired by Nintendo and hopes to eventually become a part of Nintendo to help out with future games and bring joy to those who play video games that he helped create! In the meantime, he wanted to create his own video games to get a better understanding of how they are created. Starting with small projects in class and slowly growing to bigger and much better games!
